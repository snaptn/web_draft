SNAP Basics

What is SNAP?

SNAP (South Nashville Action People) is a neighborhood organization dedicated to preserving and improving the Wedgewood-Houston neighborhood. We work to maintain the residential character of our community while supporting responsible development and community engagement.

What area does SNAP cover?

SNAP represents the Wedgewood-Houston neighborhood, which is bounded by I-65 to the west, Chestnut Street to the north, 8th Avenue South to the east, and the CSX railroad tracks to the south.

Financial & Transparency

How are SNAP's funds used?

SNAP's funds are used to support community initiatives, maintain the community center, organize neighborhood events, and fund projects that benefit the entire community. All financial decisions are made transparently and with community input.

How can I view SNAP's financial records?

SNAP's financial records are available to all community members. You can request to view them at any community meeting or by contacting the board directly.

Community Center

How can I book the community center?

The SNAP Community Center can be booked for community events and meetings. To book the space, please contact us through the contact form or attend a community meeting to discuss your event.

What facilities are available at the community center?

The community center includes a main meeting room, kitchen facilities, and outdoor space. The center is available for community meetings, events, and activities that benefit the neighborhood.

Getting Involved

How can I get involved with SNAP?

There are many ways to get involved with SNAP! You can attend community meetings, participate in neighborhood events, volunteer for community projects, or join one of our committees. All neighbors are welcome to participate.

When are community meetings held?

Community meetings are held on the second Tuesday of every other month at 6:30 PM at the SNAP Community Center. All neighbors are welcome to attend and participate in discussions about neighborhood issues and initiatives.
