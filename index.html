About SNAP

The SNAP Neighborhood Association is dedicated to fostering a vibrant, inclusive community where neighbors connect, support each other, and work together to make our neighborhood a better place to live.

Through regular events, community projects, and collaborative initiatives, we strive to build lasting relationships and create a strong sense of belonging for all residents.
